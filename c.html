Workouts

Below are simple routines grouped by goal. Click each card to reveal steps.

Full-Body Beginner Circuit

Goal: General fitness

  • 10 squats
  • 10 push-ups (knees ok)
  • 20 second plank
  • Repeat 3 times

Cardio Blast

Goal: Improve heart health

  • 30 seconds jumping jacks
  • 30 seconds high knees
  • 60 second brisk walk
  • Repeat 4 cycles

Flexibility & Mobility

Goal: Improve flexibility

  • 10 hamstring stretches (30s each)
  • 10 shoulder rolls
  • Deep breathing and cool down

Backed by general fitness tips. Always consult a trusted adult or professional before starting new exercises.
