Upper Body Exercises

Build strength and muscle in your chest, back, shoulders, and arms with these effective upper body exercises. Equipment will be need to perform some of these exercises.

Push-ups

A fundamental bodyweight exercise that targets chest, shoulders, and triceps.

How to Perform
  1. Start in a plank position with hands shoulder-width apart.
  2. Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor.
  3. Push back up to the starting position.
  4. Repeat for desired number of repetition.
Muscles Worked
  • Chest (pectorals)
  • Shoulders (deltoids)
  • Triceps
  • support your whole core

3 sets × 10–15 reps • Beginner friendly • No equipment needed

Dumbbell Rows

Used for building strength and muscle.

How to Perform
  1. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with a neutral grip.
  2. Bend forward at the hips, keeping your back straight.
  3. Row the dumbbells toward your hips, squeezing your shoulder blades together.
  4. Lower the dumbbells in a controlled manner.
Muscles Worked
  • Back (lats and rhomboids)
  • Biceps
  • Shoulders
  • Core and lower back (stabilizers)

3 sets × 8–12 reps • Intermediate • Requires dumbbells

Pull-ups

A challenging vertical pulling exercise that builds back and arm strength.

How to Perform
  1. Grip the pull-up bar with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
  2. Pull yourself up until your chin is above the bar.
  3. Lower yourself back down in a controlled manner.
  4. Repeat for desired number of reps.
Muscles Worked
  • Back (lats)
  • Biceps
  • Shoulders
  • Forearms and grip strength

3-4 sets × 5–10 reps • Advanced • Requires pull-up bar

Shoulder Press

An overhead pressing movement that targets shoulders and strengthens the upper body.

How to Perform
  1. Hold dumbbells at shoulder height with elbows bent.
  2. Press the dumbbells straight overhead until arms are extended.
  3. Lower the dumbbells back to shoulder height in a controlled motion.
  4. Repeat for desired number of reps.
Muscles Worked
  • Shoulders (deltoids)
  • Triceps
  • Upper chest
  • Core stabilizers

3 sets × 8–12 reps • Intermediate • Requires dumbbells

Bicep Curls

An isolated arm exercise perfect for building bicep size and strength.

How to Perform
  1. Hold dumbbells with arms at your sides and palms facing forward.
  2. Bend your elbows and curl the dumbbells toward your shoulders.
  3. Squeeze your biceps at the top of the movement.
  4. Lower the dumbbells back to the starting position in a controlled manner.
Muscles Worked
  • Biceps
  • Forearms
  • Brachialis

3 sets × 12–15 reps • Beginner friendly • Requires dumbbells
